## 1. Brand Overview

### Who We Are

Golden Stone QLD is a Brisbane-based stone masonry business supplying and installing kitchen benchtops in natural stone (granite, marble, quartzite) and engineered stone across South-East Queensland. We work with joineries and home owners across Brisbane, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, and Toowoomba.

### What We Do

- Cut, finish, and install kitchen benchtops
- Natural stone: granite, marble, quartzite
- Engineered stone: including silica-free options compliant with Australia's federal ban effective 1 July 2024
- Direct supply to joineries (B2B) and home owners (B2C)

### Why We Are Different

- **Experience**: Our manager brings over 15 years of stone masonry expertise to every job
- **Speed**: Fast quotes (within 24 hours) and reliable installation scheduling
- **Silica-free compliance**: Silica-free engineered stone available — protecting tradespeople and staying ahead of regulation
- **Volume capability**: Personal service at scale — joineries get the same care as private clients
- **Coverage**: One team serving all of South-East QLD

### Mission

To deliver professional benchtop installation that joineries can rely on and home owners can feel confident about — on time, at the right price, with no surprises.

## 2. Positioning

### Target Audiences

**Primary — B2B (Joineries)**
Kitchen cabinet manufacturers and custom joinery businesses that outsource benchtop supply and installation. They need a partner, not just a supplier.

- Pain points: subcontractors who miss deadlines, inconsistent quality, hard to communicate with
- Priorities: reliable scheduling, competitive pricing, easy ordering, repeat-friendly relationship

**Secondary — B2C (Home Owners)**
Owners of new-build or renovation projects at the mid-range of the market. They're investing in their home, not looking for luxury — they want clarity, speed, and a good result.

- Pain points: confusing choices, slow quotes, tradespeople who don't communicate
- Priorities: clear advice, fast quote, professional finish, no unexpected delays

### Positioning Statement

Golden Stone QLD is the stone masonry partner for South-East Queensland joineries and home owners who need professional benchtop installation — delivered reliably, quoted fast, and backed by real experience.

### Tagline Options

Three options for testing and selection:

1. **"Cut to standard. Installed on time."** — B2B-forward, efficiency-focused, literal industry confidence
2. **"Stone done right."** — Short, confident, works across both audiences, plain Australian
3. **"Your stone, our expertise."** — Inclusive, positions the customer's vision with our execution

*Recommended for primary use: **"Stone done right."** — versatile, memorable, direct, zero cliché.*

### Concepts

**Concept A — GS Monogram Badge**

A geometric navy square badge containing the interlocking "GS" letters — G in white, S in Steel Blue — paired with the full wordmark. The badge reads confidently at small sizes (favicon, uniform embroidery, van signage). The vertical rule between G and S hints at precision cutting.

Files:
- `logos/concept-a-wordmark.svg` — full colour
- `logos/concept-a-mono.svg` — monochrome (black)
- `logos/concept-a-inverted.svg` — white on dark

**Concept B — Precision Cut Diamond**

A bisected diamond mark: the top half in Slate Navy, the bottom half in Steel Blue, divided by a clean white horizontal cut line. The mark abstracts the act of precision stone cutting into a simple, modern geometric symbol. Strong at all scales.

Files:
- `logos/concept-b-wordmark.svg` — full colour
- `logos/concept-b-mono.svg` — monochrome (black/grey)
- `logos/concept-b-inverted.svg` — white/light on dark

### Which Concept to Use Where

| Context | Recommended |
|---------|-------------|
| Website header | Either — decide on one and commit |
| Email signature | Concept A (monogram reads well small) |
| Van/vehicle wrap | Concept B (diamond mark has visual impact at distance) |
| Uniform embroidery | Concept A (text badge embroiders cleanly) |
| Social media avatar | Concept B mark only (diamond crops to circle well) |
| Document letterhead | Either, full colour |

### Logo Usage Rules

**Clear Space**
Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the badge/mark on all sides.

**Minimum Digital Size**
- Full logo (mark + wordmark): 200px wide minimum
- Mark only: 32px minimum

**Approved Background Combinations**

| Background | Use this variant |
|------------|-----------------|
| White / Off White `#F7F5F2` | Full colour |
| Light grey surfaces | Full colour |
| Slate Navy `#2B3A52` | Inverted (white) |
| Dark photography | Inverted (white) |
| Single-colour print (black) | Mono |

### Logo Don'ts

- ❌ Do not stretch or distort proportions
- ❌ Do not recolour outside the approved palette
- ❌ Do not add drop shadows, gradients, or glows
- ❌ Do not place on busy photographic backgrounds without a clear backdrop
- ❌ Do not rotate the mark
- ❌ Do not use the monogram letters in a different typeface
- ❌ Do not recreate the logo in Word, PowerPoint, or similar — use the SVG files

## 6. Brand Voice & Tone

### Brand Personality

The Golden Stone QLD voice sits at these points on each spectrum:

```
Formal ←─────────────────●────→ Casual          (leaning professional, not stiff)
Simple ←●──────────────────────→ Complex         (plain English, trade-competent)
Serious ←────────●─────────────→ Playful         (reliable, not humourless)
Reserved ←────●────────────────→ Expressive      (confident, not loud)
```

### Voice Principles

---

#### Principle 1 — Be direct. Say what you mean.

The brand doesn't dress things up. No padding, no filler, no jargon for its own sake. If something takes 2 days, say 2 days.

**B2B version — Joineries:**
> **Say this:** "Turnaround is typically 5–7 business days from template. We'll confirm your slot when the order is placed."
> **Not this:** "We strive to deliver an efficient and streamlined scheduling experience tailored to your workflow needs."

**B2C version — Home Owners:**
> **Say this:** "We'll measure up, give you a quote within 24 hours, and lock in your install date — usually within 2 weeks."
> **Not this:** "Our bespoke consultation process ensures a tailored journey from concept through to completion."

---

#### Principle 2 — Lead with experience, not ego.

15 years in the trade is the proof. Let it show in how you explain things, not in how much you talk about yourself.

**B2B version — Joineries:**
> **Say this:** "We've run high-volume joinery programs before. Send us your specs and we'll price it properly."
> **Not this:** "As industry-leading stone masonry specialists, our unparalleled expertise sets us apart."

**B2C version — Home Owners:**
> **Say this:** "Not sure whether to go with marble or quartzite? We'll walk you through the real trade-offs for your kitchen."
> **Not this:** "Our passionate craftspeople are here to guide you on a journey of stone discovery."

---

#### Principle 3 — Compliance is a feature, not a lecture.

Silica-free engineered stone is a genuine advantage. Mention it where relevant — when customers ask about engineered stone, in material comparisons, on product pages. Don't lead with it everywhere; don't hide it either.

**B2B version — Joineries:**
> **Say this:** "All our engineered stone is silica-free and compliant with the federal ban in force since July 2024."
> **Not this:** "In response to the significant regulatory developments pertaining to crystalline silica exposure risk..."

**B2C version — Home Owners:**
> **Say this:** "We only supply silica-free engineered stone — it's safer for the tradespeople installing it, and it meets current Australian law."
> **Not this:** "We are proud to be at the forefront of the silica-safe movement in the Australian stone industry."

---

#### Principle 4 — Price confidence, not apology.

The brand serves the mid-market. Don't pretend to be luxury; don't position as bargain-basement. Competitive pricing is a strength — say it plainly.

**B2B version — Joineries:**
> **Say this:** "Our pricing is structured for volume joinery work. The more consistent the workflow, the better the rate we can offer."
> **Not this:** "We offer cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality."

**B2C version — Home Owners:**
> **Say this:** "Good stone work doesn't have to cost a fortune. Get a quote — most standard kitchens come in well under what people expect."
> **Not this:** "We believe premium quality should be accessible to everyone."

---

#### Principle 5 — Queensland, not the world.

This is a local business serving South-East QLD. Own that. Regional specificity builds trust.

> **Say this:** "We cover Brisbane, Gold Coast, Sunshine Coast, and Toowoomba — no surcharges within the service area."
> **Not this:** "We service all areas" or "available Australia-wide."

---

### Prohibited Terms and Phrases

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| bespoke | Signals luxury/boutique, doesn't match the positioning |
| exclusive | Same reason |
| artisanal craftsmanship | Rustic cliché, wrong register |
| timeless | Overused, vague |
| heritage craft | Same — wrong tone |
| unparalleled / industry-leading | Hollow superlative |
| journey | Marketing filler |
| passionate about stone | Every tradesperson says this |
| cost-effective solutions | Corporate non-speak |
| we strive to | Weak; just say what you do |

## 8. Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style

- **Lighting**: Natural, even light. No dramatic moody shadows — the material should be visible, not atmospheric.
- **Subjects**: Stone close-ups showing texture and grain; installation in progress; finished kitchen scenes at mid-range not luxury renovation; the team at work.
- **Colour treatment**: True-to-life. No heavy filters, no faded presets. The stone colours should look accurate so customers know what to expect.
- **Perspective**: Eye-level or slight downward angle for benchtops. No forced wide-angle distortion.

### What to Avoid in Imagery

- Stock images of impossibly perfect kitchens
- Heavily styled editorial shots (wrong register for the brand)
- Moody/dark atmospheric photography (more appropriate for a luxury brand)
- Images that could be from any stone company in the world — aim for shots that feel specifically Queensland/Australian

### Icons and Illustrations

- Style: Outlined, clean, 2px stroke weight
- Corner radius: 2–4px (not perfectly sharp, not heavily rounded)
- Colour: Slate Navy or Steel Blue only; never multi-colour icon sets
- Avoid decorative illustrations — this is a trade business, not a consumer lifestyle brand
